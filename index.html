Private Projects

Staff Clockin System

The Staff Clockin System is a private project designed to help businesses efficiently record employee clock-in and clock-out times. This system enables businesses to export records into neatly formatted timesheets in Excel, ensuring streamlined time management and payroll processing.

Recruitment Agency Website

I developed a comprehensive website for a recruitment agency, showcasing their services to attract potential clients. The website includes a job posting function, allowing the agency to post vacancies and manage applications seamlessly.

Cleaning Company Website

I created a user-friendly website for a cleaning company, enabling customers to book cleaning services online with an instant estimate quote feature. The website integrates with a Telegram bot to notify employees of new bookings, enhancing operational efficiency.

Automate Data Entry Software

I developed an automated data entry software for the finance department to streamline the process of transferring worker data from Excel spreadsheets into government tax PDF forms. The software accurately reads the Excel file and inputs data into the correct fields on the PDF form, saving time and reducing errors.

Sticker Business Website

Utilizing the Shopify eCommerce platform, I developed a website for a custom sticker business. The site allows users to order and purchase custom stickers online. I customized the Liquid theme to meet the specific needs of the client, ensuring a personalized shopping experience.

High Visibility Retail Website

I built a Shopify-based website for a business selling workwear clothing. The site provides a platform for customers to order and purchase high-visibility workwear. I tailored the Liquid theme to align with the client's requirements, offering a customized and professional online store.

Jumping Castle Website
